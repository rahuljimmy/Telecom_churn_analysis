# 📊 Telecom Customer Churn Analysis (Python EDA Project)

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by telecom companies. Retaining existing customers is often more cost-effective than acquiring new ones. This project focuses on performing **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a Telecom Customer Churn dataset to uncover key patterns, trends, and factors influencing customer churn.

Through detailed analysis and visualizations, this project identifies important business insights related to customer demographics, internet services, billing information, contracts, payment methods, tenure, and customer support services.

The ultimate goal of this project is to help telecom companies understand customer behavior and take proactive measures to improve customer retention.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer churn behavior in the telecom industry
* Identify the major factors contributing to customer churn
* Explore customer demographics and service usage patterns
* Understand how billing, contracts, and support services affect churn
* Generate actionable business insights for customer retention
* Build a strong foundation for future churn prediction models

# 💡 Key Insights

### 🔴 Customers with Month-to-Month Contracts Churn More

Customers with month-to-month contracts showed significantly higher churn rates compared to customers with long-term contracts.

### 🔴 Fiber Optic Customers Tend to Churn More

Fiber optic internet users exhibited higher churn behavior, possibly due to pricing or service expectations.

### 🔴 Higher Monthly Charges Increase Churn Probability

Customers with higher monthly charges were more likely to churn.

### 🔴 Customers with Low Tenure Churn More Frequently

New customers showed a higher tendency to leave the service.

### 🔴 Lack of Tech Support and Online Security Increases Churn

Customers without additional support services were more likely to churn.

### 🔴 Senior Citizens Have Higher Churn Rates

Senior citizens were observed to churn at a much higher rate compared to non-senior customers.

### 🔴 Customers with Dependents Tend to Stay Longer

Customers with dependents showed better retention behavior.

# 🚀 Business Recommendations

### ✅ Improve Retention for Month-to-Month Customers

### ✅ Reduce Churn Among Senior Citizens

* Offer senior citizen discounts

### ✅ Improve Fiber Optic Customer Satisfaction

* Review pricing strategies
* Improve internet service quality

### ✅ Encourage Customers to Use Tech Support Services

* Promote technical support plans
* Provide awareness campaigns about security services

### ✅ Reduce Early Customer Churn
